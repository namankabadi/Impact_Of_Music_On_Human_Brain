Data Description

The recorded EEG data used in this project were carefully cleaned to ensure the removal of artifacts and to maintain the integrity of the signals for further analysis. The process involved:

  • Cleaning: Artifacts were removed from the raw EEG data to preserve the signal quality.
  • Epoching: The continuous EEG data was segmented into epochs, time-locked to the onset and conclusion of the musical exposure.
  • Baseline Correction: Each epoch underwent baseline correction by subtracting the mean pre-stimulus signal.

The final cleaned and processed EEG data is available for download in the form of a zipped folder containing all relevant CSV files.

Please note that all data in this project is the original work of the authors as mentioned in the about section of our website. The data can only be used for understanding and advancing research in the field, and any further use should be accompanied by proper citation of our paper.
